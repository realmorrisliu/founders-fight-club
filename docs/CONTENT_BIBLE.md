## 1. Direction Lock

- Naming: near-real parody names (recognizable in one glance).
- Tone: meme-forward satire, playful rather than dark.
- Skill identity: special skills are product/company driven.
- Launch target: 15 standard fighters + 1 legend boss.

## 2. Naming & Safety Rules

- Slightly alter real names (one letter/syllable) for parody distance.
- Do not use exact real logos or trademark brandmarks in UI.
- Keep visual language caricatured and stylized, not photo-real.
- Add disclaimer in menu/README:
  - "Satirical fictional game. Not affiliated with real persons or companies."

## 3. Combat Model

Current layer (already implemented):
- `light / heavy / special / throw`

Launch content layer (for identity):
- `Signature A` (neutral + special)
- `Signature B` (forward + special)
- `Signature C` (down + special)
- `Hype Ultimate` (full meter, cinematic)

## 5. Story Structure (16-Roster Arcade)

1. Cold open: `Elon Mvsk` and `Mark Zuck` ignite the octagon meme war.
2. Qualifier ladder: random founder bracket introduces product-themed fighters.
3. Mid-arc crisis: `Peter Thyell` vs `Sam Altmyn` board-coup event.
4. Main event split:
   - Route A: beat `Elon Mvsk` then `Mark Zuck`,
   - Route B: beat `Mark Zuck` then `Elon Mvsk`.
5. Final founder title match against a rotating top-seed (`Jensen Hwang` / `Sundar Pichoy` / `Zef Bezos`).
6. Legend encounter: `Steve Jobz` boss unlocks as final ideology duel.
7. Ending choice:
   - `Open Stack`,
   - `Walled Garden`.
